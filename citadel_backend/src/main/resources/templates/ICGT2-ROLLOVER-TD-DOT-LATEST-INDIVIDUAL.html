TRUST DEED (INDIVIDUAL)

THIS TRUST DEED is made on this ${agreementSignedDate} (hereinafter referred to as “Deed”) and sets out the terms and conditions which the party described in Section 1 of Schedule A attached hereto (hereinafter called “Settlor”) on the one part remit such amount of money (hereinafter called “Trust Fund”) upon CITADEL TRUSTEE BERHAD [Company No.: 202001010278 (1366598-H)], a public limited company incorporated in Malaysia under the Companies Act 2016 and registered as a Trust Company under the Trust Companies Act 1949 and having its registered office and place of business at L3-1, Third Floor, Wisma LYL, No. 12, Jalan 51A/223, 46100 Petaling Jaya, Selangor (hereinafter referred to as “Trustee”) establishing a trust (hereinafter called “Trust”).

RECITALS

WHEREAS

  1. The Settlor desires to establish a trust for the benefit of the several person(s) identified by the Settlor whereby such persons agree to be bound by the provisions of this Deed for the benefit of the party described in Section 2 of Schedule A attached hereto (hereinafter collectively referred to as the “Beneficiary").

  2. The Trustee is a trust company incorporated pursuant to Trust Companies Act 1949 and is authorized to conduct any of the activities listed under Section 8 of the Trust Companies Act 1949.

  3. The Settlor is desirous to appoint the Trustee to act as a trustee of this Trust to manage and administer the Trust Fund in accordance with the terms and conditions appearing hereinafter and the Trustee is agreeable to accept such appointment.

  4. The Settlor and the Trustee shall be referred to separately as “Party” and collectively as the “Parties”.

NOW, THEREFORE, IT IS HEREBY AGREED BY THE PARTIES AS FOLLOWS: -

Definitions and Interpretations

1. 

TRUST

1.1 

The purpose of this Deed is to establish the general covenants between the Settlor and the Trustee in establishing a Trust under each Declaration of Trust.

1.2 

Each Declaration of Trust comprises a separate agreement for the establishment of Trust described in a Declaration of Trust and as such each Trust shall commence two (2) years from the date of each Declaration of Trust subject to the determination of Trust as provided in clause 8 of this Deed, unless otherwise expressly provided in the Declaration of Trust (hereinafter referred to as the “Duration of Trust").

1.3 

The purpose of the Trust is for the Trustee to hold on trust the Trust Fund on behalf of the Settlor and to deal with all of the earnings, avails and proceeds derived of the Trust Fund as per the provisions of this Deed and/or the instructions of the Settlor for the Duration of Trust for the benefit of the Beneficiary.

2.  BENEFICIARY

2.1 

The Beneficiary of this Trust shall be the persons identified in each Declaration of Trust and as described in Section 2 of Schedule A attached hereto, who shall abide by the terms and conditions of this Deed during the tenure of the Trust for as long as they remain as Beneficiary.

2.2 

Upon the instruction of the Settlor, the Trustee may at any time appoint additional person(s) as Beneficiary or remove any of the existing Beneficiary from the benefit under the Trust by providing written notice to the Trustee and such change shall be effective within three (3) working days from the date the Trustee receives such notice.

2.3 

In the event of the death or the dissolution of any of the Beneficiary, the share of such Beneficiary would be distributed to the remaining Beneficiary (after the completion of the tenure) or if there is more than one Beneficiary, equally between the remaining Beneficiary. For avoidance of doubt, the death or dissolution of any one of the Beneficiary shall not terminate this Trust or in any manner affect the powers of the Trustee.

3.  ADMINISTRATION OF THE TRUST

3.1 

Unless the Settlor expressly appoints a third party (hereinafter referred to as the “Advisor/Administrator”) to manage and administer the Trust Fund in the Declaration of Trust, the Trustee shall have the full discretion to manage and administer the Trust Fund for the benefit of the Beneficiary.

4.  TRUSTEE POWERS

4.1 

Trustees have the discretion (after proper consideration) whether or not to use the power as it varies according to the character of the trust. If powers are used or unused upon proper consideration, the beneficiary will not be allowed to complain. In administrating the Trust and in facilitating the management of the Trust in accordance to Clause 3.1 of the Deed, the Trustee shall have the following powers: -

a) 

all the powers conferred under the Trustee Act 1949;

b) 

to do all things and perform all acts which in the Trustee’s judgment is necessary and proper for the protection of the Trust Fund;

c) 

to hold and continue to hold on trust the Trust Fund on behalf of the Settlor, so long as they deem proper, and to place and replace in the authorized placement including any assets purchased pursuant to the authorized placement;

d) 

to make, execute, acknowledge, and deliver all deeds, releases, mortgages, leases, contracts, agreements, instruments, and other obligations of whatsoever nature relating to the Trust Fund, and generally to have full right, power and authority to do all things and perform all acts necessary to make the instruments proper and legal;

e) 

to compromise, settle, arbitrate, or defend any claim or demand in favor of or against the Trust;

f) 

to incur and pay the ordinary and necessary expenses of administration, including (but not by way of limitation) reasonable attorneys' fees, accountants' fees, placement counsel fees, and the like;

g) 

to act through an agent or attorney-in-fact, by and under power of attorney duly executed by the Trustee, in carrying out any of the authorized powers and duties;

h) 

to represent the Trust and the Beneficiary in all suits and legal proceedings relating to the Trust Fund in any court of law of equity, or before any other bodies or tribunals; to initiate suits and to prosecute them to final judgment or decree; to compromise claims or suits, and to submit the same to arbitration when, in his judgment, such course is necessary or proper;

i) 

to pay all lawful taxes and assessments and the necessary expenses of the Trust and make payment of any and all other legitimate expenses of the Trust from the Trust Fund;

j) 

to collect notes, obligations, dividends, and all other payments that may be due and payable to the Trust; to deposit the proceeds thereof, as well as any other moneys from whatsoever source they may be derived, in any suitable bank or depository, and to draw the same from time to time;

k) 

appoint any one or more of its own officers as its attorney (jointly and severally if more than one) with power to execute documents on behalf of the Trustee for the day to day running of the Trust; and

i) 

engage and pay, at the expense of the Trust, for the advice or services of any banker, banking company, lawyer, accountant or any other professional advisers or experts whose advice or services may be reasonably necessary and rely and act upon any advice so obtained for the performance of their respective duties and services hereunder and shall incur no liability for action taken or suffered to be taken or omitted to be taken in good faith and in accordance with the opinion or advice of such any banker, banking company, lawyer, accountant and any other professional advisers or experts.

5.  TRUSTEE FEE

5.1 

In consideration of the services provided by the Trustee, the Trustee may charge a fee for such amount as may be prescribed by the Trustee from time to time (hereinafter referred to as the “Trustee Fee”) of which the Trustee is entitled to reimburse themselves with the Trustee Fee from the Trust Fund.

5.2 

In the event that the Trust Fund is insufficient towards the settlement of the Trustee Fees, and the Trustee Fee remain outstanding and due unless otherwise prohibited by law, the Trustee shall have lien over the Trust Fund with the power to sell any or all the assets under the Trust in or towards settlement of the outstanding amount.

6.  DUTIES OF TRUSTEES

6.1 

It shall be the duty of Trustee in addition to the other duties herein imposed upon the Trustee: -

a) 

to maintain a complete record of all the necessary details of the Beneficiary including the name and residence of the Beneficiary and such other details as the Trustee may deem fit;

b) 

to maintain careful and accurate books showing the transactions ie. receipts and disbursements relating to the Trust and also of the Trust Fund and such other details as the Trustee may deem fit;

c) 

to maintain books of the Trust open for inspection by the Beneficiary and/or Settlor at such reasonable times at the main office of the Trustee;r other Shariah-compliant structures as approved by the Shariah Adviser;

d) 

to furnish the Beneficiary at special meetings the financial standing of the Trust and of such other account or information concerning the affairs of the Trust as the Beneficiary shall request.

e) 

To safeguard the Settlor’s assets as to ensure that the general principles and prescriptive requirements are consistently adhered.

7.  DISTRIBUTION OF TRUST FUND

7.1 

In the absence of an Advisor/Administrator being appointed by the Settlor, earnings from the holding of the Trust Fund and/or any proceed receives from the Trust Fund (if any) shall be properly and/or equally distributed to the Beneficiary from time to time in accordance with the agreed proportion as described under the respective Declaration of Trust and as described in Section 2 of Schedule A attached hereto of this Deed.

7.2 

Notwithstanding anything herein, the Trust Fund is subjected to costs and expenses, directly and indirectly, incurred in connection to the establishment, management, operation and execution of the Trust including but not limited to any payment of any and all tax liabilities and Trustee Fee including any stamp duties, applicable taxes or charges payable by the Trustee for which the Trustee may be made liable in respect of such proper distribution (hereinafter referred to as “Trust Expenses”) and as such, before making any proper distribution to the Beneficiary, the Trustee may make deduction for the Trust Expenses from the Trust Fund.

7.3 

In the event that an Advisor/Administrator is appointed by the Settlor as provided in the Declaration of Trust, Trustee shall not be responsible for the Trust Fund from the date the Trust Fund is disbursed to the Advisor and as such Trustee shall not be responsible to the distribution of any earnings from the holding of the Trust Fund and/or any proceed receives from the Trust Funds as well as the management and administration of the Trust Fund thereafter.

8.  DETERMINATION OF TRUST

8.1 

This Trust shall be determined upon the full disbursement of the Trust Fund except in unexpected circumstances to the Settlor, Beneficiary and/or the Administrator, as the case may be.

8.2 

Upon termination of the Trust, the Trustee shall return the Trust Fund that is under the management of the Trustee prior to the termination (if any) to the Settlor or the Beneficiary, as the case may be.

8.3 

In the event that an Advisor/Administrator is appointed by the Settlor in accordance to clause 3.1 above, upon the termination of the Trust, the Trustee shall not be responsible to transfer or cause to return the Trust Fund that have been disbursed by the Trustee in accordance with clause 3.1 above or to the Advisor/Administrator (as the case may be) and the Settlor understands and agrees that that the Settlor is personally responsible to commence any claims on such Trust Fund that have been disbursed to the Advisor/Administrator.

9.  NOTICES

9.1 

Any notice or other document to be given by either Party under the Agreement shall be deemed to be properly given with the other Party’s full knowledge of its contents if sent by hand, registered post or facsimile to the relevant telecommunications numbers and addresses or such numbers or addresses as the parties may from time to time designated by notice.

9.2 

Every notice required or permitted to be given or made hereunder shall be deemed to have been received when:

a) 

in the case of delivery by hand, when delivered; or

b) 

in the case of prepaid post, on the second (2nd ) day following the day of posting; or

c) 

in the case of facsimile, on acknowledgement of the addressee's facsimile receiving equipment (where such acknowledgement occurs before 5:00 pm on the day of acknowledgement) and in any other case on the day following the day of acknowledgement.

9.3 

Any notice or other communication not received on a Business Day or received after 5:00p.m. local time on any Business Day shall be deemed to be received on the next following Business Day. No change of in the address of the Parties howsoever brought about shall be effective or binding on either Party unless that Party has given to the other actual notice of such change of address and nothing done in reliance on this Clause 11 shall be affected or prejudice by any subsequent change in the address of one Party which the other Party has no actual knowledge of at the time act or thing was done or carried out.

10.  COST AND EXPENSES

10.1 

All costs incidental to this Deed shall be borne and paid by the Settlor including the stamp duty and solicitors’ costs of the Trustee in connection with the preparation, stamping, registration and execution hereof.

11.  CONFIDENTIALITY

11.1 

All information in relation to this Deed shall be treated as strictly confidential and Parties agrees not disclose any confidential information received during the course of this Deed without prior written consent of the other Party, save and except to its auditors, legal counsel and other professional advisors and as may be necessary in the discharge of the duties pursuant to this Deed, where disclosure is necessary to any regulators or to any persons pursuant to any written law or regulation (local or foreign) or where such information is already available in public domain.

12.  LIABILITY AND INDEMNITY

12.1 

The Settlor shall at all times hereafter indemnify, defend and hold harmless the Trustee and continue to indemnify Trustee against all actions, proceedings, claims, demands, penalties, costs (including legal costs) and expenses which may be brought or made against or incurred by the Trustee by reason or on account of Trustee adhering to the Settlor’s instruction and executing this Trust within its mandate.

12.2 

This Clause shall survive the determination of this Deed and continue for so long as is necessary to give its full effect, notwithstanding any other provision in this Deed.

12.3 

In the professed execution of the Trust and powers of the Trustee, the Trustee and its agents shall not be liable for any loss by reason of any improper investments made in good faith or the carrying on of any business in good faith or in connection with the administration and management of the Trust by reason of any mistake or omission made in good faith by the Trustee unless it is caused by its own gross negligence or by commission of a willful act of breach of trust.

12.4 

The Trustee may validly act upon the opinion or advice of or information obtained from advocates and solicitors whether instructed by the Trustee and/or the Settlor and the Trustee may act upon any statements of or information obtained from the bankers, accountants, valuers and other persons appointed by the Trustee believed by the Trustee in good faith to be an expert in relation to the matters upon which they are consulted and the Trustee shall not be liable for anything done or suffered by it in good faith in reliance upon such opinion, advice, statements or information provide that any such bankers, accountants, valuers and other persons consulted.

The Trustee undertakes not to transfer such Personal Data other than for the purpose of the execution of this Trust and to take reasonable measures to ensure that the Personal Data is secure.

15.1 

This Deed and all disputes and differences arising hereunder shall be governed by and interpreted in according with the laws for the time being in force in Malaysia. The Parties hereby agree to submit to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of Malaysia in respect of any claim or proceeding arising from this Deed and to obey any order or judgment of the courts in respect of such claim or proceedings.

16.  LANGUAGE

17.1 

All communications, reports, data, instructions and proceedings of whatsoever kind arising under or in relation to this Deed shall be made in English.

17.  FORCE MAJEURE

17.1 

The Trustee shall not be in breach of any obligations under the Deed or any part thereof as a result of an occurrence of an event of Force Majeure. The performance of any obligation or obligations suspended while Force Majeure is operative shall be resumed as soon as such Force Majeure event ceases. Any loss or damage or delays in, or failure of performance by the Trustee hereto shall not give rise to any claims for damages or loss of anticipated profits, if and to the extent that such loss, damage, delay or failure is caused by Force Majeure.

17.2 

Force Majeure means anything outside the Trustee’s reasonable control and includes but is not limited to fire, storm, flood, earthquake or other acts of God, explosion, accident, act of public enemy or terrorists, war, rebellion, insurrection, sabotage, epidemic, quarantine restriction, labour dispute, transportation embargo, change of laws effected by governmental authority.

17.3 

In the event of a Force Majeure situation which hinders the Trustee from discharging its obligations under the Deed, the Trustee shall promptly notify the Settlor giving the estimated extent and duration of such inability to perform its obligations hereunder and other reasonable full particulars and shall use its best endeavours to remedy the situation.

17.4 

In the event that an event of Force Majeure frustrates the original intentions and objectives of the Deed or any part thereof, The Trustee shall have the right to revoke the Deed or any part thereof by seven (7) days written notice served on the other Party and the legal and beneficial title of all Trust Fund including any physical delivery, if applicable, shall be transferred to the Beneficiary in accordance to this Deed.

18.  NO PARTNERSHIP

18.1 

Nothing in this Deed shall be construed as creating a partnership between the Parties or giving rise to any duties (fiduciary or otherwise) other than those expressly stipulated hereunder. Neither Party shall have any right, power or authority to enter into any agreement or commitment, act on behalf of, or otherwise bind the other Party in any way.

19.  COUNTERPARTS

19.1 

The Deed may be executed in any number of counterparts or duplicates each of which shall be on original, but such counterparts or duplicates shall together constitute but one and the same deed and shall come into effect on the date first here-in-above mentioned irrespective of the diverse dates upon which the Parties may have executed the Deed.

20.  ENTIRE DEED

20.1 

This Deed incorporated herein constitutes and represents the entire understanding and agreement between the Parties hereto and shall supersede any and all prior Deeds, whether written or oral, that may exist between the Parties regarding the same.

21.  TIME

21.1 

Time whenever mentioned shall be deemed to be treated as the essence of this Deed. If unspecified, the time constraint shall be deemed as that of a reasonable time.

22.  SEVERABILITY

22.1 

If at any time any provision, condition or term stipulated in this Deed is or become illegal, void, invalid, prohibited or unenforceable in any respect, the same shall be ineffective to the extent of such illegality, invalidity, prohibition or unenforceability without invalidating in any manner the remaining provisions thereof.

23.  EARLY REDEMPTION OF TRUST FUND

23.1

However, should the client request for early redemption due to emergencies, a penalty of 25% of the capital will be applied provided the withdrawal is made after 18 months of the tenure.
